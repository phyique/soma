About Us

For over fourteen years, Precision Graphics has provided excellent quality printing and on time delivery to all our customers. Established in 1997, the growth and development of our company has been ably nurtured and developed by Patrick Wynter and his dedicated and experienced team. We understand the need for fast turnaround and Precision delivers on time, every time. We utilize a variety of machinery to ensure your satisfaction, such as single and multi-coloured presses capable of producing the highest quality lithography. We are especially proud of our five-colour press, a sophisticated machine guaranteed to produce work worthy of a double-take.

Our presses are supported by computer controls to reduce printing time and improve overall quality. Supporting our five colour Heidelberg printing Press is a modern state-of-the-art Computer to Plate (CTP) system in our pre-press department. This enables us to reduce our pre-press operation time and improve the quality of our output delivery.

To complement our high quality full colour printing we are equipped with a modern bindery with state of the art MBO folding machines, programmed Polar cutter, and Horizon perfect binder. This equipment gurantees quality and accuracy for all our jobs big and small.

Our state-of-the-art printing equipment ensures that we deliver. Leave the hassle to us!
